About Undertale

Undertale is an indie role-playing game developed by Toby Fox. It was released in 2015 for Microsoft Windows, macOS, and Linux, and later for PlayStation 4, PlayStation Vita, and Nintendo Switch.

The game features a unique combat system where players can choose to spare or attack monsters, leading to different outcomes and multiple endings. Its engaging story, memorable characters, and innovative gameplay have earned it widespread acclaim.

Developer: Toby Fox

Toby Fox is an American game designer, programmer, and composer known for creating Undertale and Deltarune. His work is characterized by a focus on storytelling, character development, and unconventional game mechanics.

Gameplay and Reception

Undertale has been praised for its humor, characters, and the ability to complete the game without killing any enemies. The game has received numerous awards and nominations, including "Best Game" at the Game Developers Choice Awards and "Best Independent Game" at The Game Awards.
